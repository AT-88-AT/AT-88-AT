Tetris VS Simulator

Gameplay Instructions

Stack tetrominoes to fill rows and clear lines. In VS mode, clearing multiple lines sends garbage to opponent. Reach higher levels for faster drops and exotic animations.

Controls

  • WASD and ARROW buttons for movement
  • Space for hard drop
